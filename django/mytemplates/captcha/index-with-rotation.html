symCaptcha

symCaptcha is a captcha based on image symmetry. To enjoy it, take a glance at the simple instructions and examples.

  • 1. Find the edge of the main object shown in images
  • 2. Draw the symmetry line segment by simply making two clicks on the edge
  • 3. If you cannot find a symmetry, please replace the images by clicking "Try New" botton; after working on both images, click 'Submit' to see if you work it out
